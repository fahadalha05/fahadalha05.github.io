Clash Royale Page

    What is Clash Royale?

    A fast-paced real-time battle game where players use cards to deploy troops, spells, and buildings. Destroy crown towers to win!

    Elixir System

    You gain 1 elixir every 2.8 seconds. Manage elixir carefully to defend, counter-push, and overwhelm your opponent.

    Game Modes

    Includes Ladder, Path of Legends, 2v2, Challenges, Global Tournaments, Training, Classic & Grand Challenges.

    Card Types

    Troops, Spells, and Buildings — each with rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Champion, and Evolution Cards.
